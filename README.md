# The AI Training Memory Vault

An invisible browser extension that permanently and dynamically injects your core context, lifestyle rules, and custom backgrounds into Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, and OpenAI. Stop re-typing your background prompts every morning—let your browser handle your alignment automatically.

---

## Quick Installation Guide (Under 60 Seconds)

Because this is a developer-validated, secure Minimum Viable Product (MVP), you can run it completely free without going through the public app store. Here is how to install it on your browser:

### Step 1: Download the Package
1. Look at the files listed above on this GitHub page.
2. Click on Archive.zip.
3. On the next screen, click the Download button to save it to your computer.
4. Go to your downloads folder and Unzip/Extract the folder so you see the files (manifest.json, popup.js, etc.).

### Step 2: Load it into Chrome
1. Open a new tab in your Google Chrome browser.
2. Type chrome://extensions/ into your address bar and hit Enter.
3. In the top-right corner of that screen, flip the toggle switch that says "Developer mode" to ON.
4. In the top-left corner, click the button that says "Load unpacked".
5. Select the unzipped Memory Vault Extension folder you downloaded in Step 1.

### Step 3: Set Your Context
1. Click the small puzzle piece icon in the top right corner of your browser.
2. Click AI Training Memory Vault to open your private, secure dark-themed control panel.
3. Type your custom lifestyle rules, target goals, or engineering context into the box and hit Quick Save.
4. Open Gemini or ChatGPT and watch your background rules seamlessly adapt into the field!
